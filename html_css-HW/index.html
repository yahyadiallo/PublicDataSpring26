EA Sports FC 24 Review

By: Yahya Diallo

Image source: Wikipedia

I’m reviewing EA Sports FC 24. Overall, it’s a solid soccer game with good graphics and realistic gameplay. The animations and player movements feel smoother compared to older FIFA games.

I like the Ultimate Team mode and Career mode, but sometimes the game can feel repetitive after a while. There’s also a big focus on spending money in Ultimate Team, which can be annoying.

Still, it’s fun to play with friends and online. I’d recommend it if you enjoy soccer games or played FIFA before.
